StackJAM Institute

General Background

StackJam is a place where overachieving and ambitious IB and AP high school scholars gather to educate others as well as gain invaluable experiences. We believe that this organization is meant to help students in need of assistance with academics; or simply those who just want to get ahead.

We offer a variety of classes, abundance of choices, and guest speakers that embody academic excellence (from Waterloo, U of T, MacMaster, Queens and more) in and outside of a practical learning environment. Our organization believes that trust, ambition, goals, persistence, trials, and integrity are one of the most important traits a student can possess. We hope to spread our beliefs among many other students and help them successfully achieve their goals in life.
